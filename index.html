Welcome

A Tradition in the Making

You are cordially invited to the inaugural Augusta Experience — an immersive Masters Sunday gathering at the Linford home. Every detail has been curated to honour the traditions of Augusta National: the atmosphere, the cuisine, the reverence, and the glory of the final round.

Guests are reminded that they are patrons of this occasion — not merely attendees. Conduct befitting the grounds is expected and appreciated at all times.

The first annual. A tradition unlike any other.

The Course of Conduct

Patron Guidelines

Augusta National has maintained a standard of conduct unmatched in sport. We ask that all patrons honour these same traditions within our grounds.

Mobile Devices

Mobile phones are not permitted in the primary viewing area. A designated patron phone zone is available in the east corridor.

Decorum

Applause is the appropriate expression of appreciation. Running, shouting, and disruptive conduct are not in keeping with the spirit of the occasion.

Mode of Address

All guests are to be addressed as Patrons. The term “fans” has no place on these grounds.

Outside Provisions

Outside food and beverages are not permitted. All provisions are available through official Augusta Experience concessions.

Young Patrons

Children are most welcome and are expected to conduct themselves in accordance with the patron standards of these grounds.

The Leaderboard

An official leaderboard will be maintained throughout the day. Patrons are encouraged to follow the action and update their brackets.

The Competitions

Activities & Events

A full programme of competition awaits. The patron who accumulates the greatest distinction across the day’s events shall be awarded the honour of donning the Green Jacket.

I

The Par 3 Contest

Pre-round competition in the backyard. Patrons of all ages welcome. Closest to the pin earns opening-day honours.

II

The Masters Pool

Select your champion before the first shot is struck. The patron whose selection claims the Green Jacket claims one of their own.

III

Putting Championship

The indoor putting green is open throughout the day. Competition format announced upon arrival. Pride is on the line.

IV

Masters Trivia

Test your knowledge of Augusta National’s history, past champions, and the traditions that define this tournament. Conducted during commercial intervals.

V

The Green Jacket Ceremony

At the conclusion of the day’s proceedings, the champion shall be recognised in an official Green Jacket presentation ceremony.

Presentation

Dress Code

Augusta National has always maintained that presentation reflects the prestige of the occasion. We ask that our patrons honour that tradition.

Gentlemen Collared shirts are required. Smart casual attire is appropriate. Hats, where worn, are to face forward.
Ladies Smart casual. You are, as always, the standard by which all others are measured.
Young Patrons Comfortable and presentable. They set the tone more than they know.
